Welcome to IronFit



What is IronFit?

IronFit is a website where you can view exercises with detailed descriptions and pictures from our library of workouts. You can also create your own list of personal fitness goals that can be updated as you complete each and every milestone. We call this the "IronFit challenge". IronFit also has a body mass index (BMI) calculator where users will be able to view their BMI by entering in their height and weight.


Why should you use IronFit?

IronFit aims to become an exercise and overall fitness hub for all. We want to expand the website to inlcude personal workout logs and nutritonal information. We also aim to foster community involvment through a forum where users can chat and leave comments on particluar workouts or dietary threads. We ask for users continued support as IronFit becomes both the fitness beginners and fitness pros one stop shop for leading a healthy lifestyle.
